16-bars: tape loops in the woods, 2024

With sound artist t l h I was commissioned to produce a tape inspired work that explored songlines within city woodlands. The final work, 16-bars, was part of a larger piece by artist Esther May Campbell, for her Anything Moving and What Remains work at the MayK festival.
