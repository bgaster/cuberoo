Muses, 2021 & 2020 & 2019

Muses was an umbrella project for work being done in the Expressive Computer Interactions Group at University of West of England (UWE). Our particular focus is applying programming language theory and practice, physical computing, networking, and musicial theory to design and implementation of Digitial Musical Instruments (DMI).

More information can be found on the projects website here.

Contributors

Muses was developed by cuberoo_ and Nathan Renney, along with contributions from Carinna Parraman and others.
