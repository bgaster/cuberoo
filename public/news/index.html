August 24, 2025

As part of a new podcast project I'm working on, called Radical Interation, we've been exploring risograph printing. At UWE we are very lucky to have access to a risograph printer and last week I got the chance to play with it. We printed out the cover for our podcast, coming soon, and also an octopus that I made in procreate, to help get a better idea of how the risograph process works and how things will look.
